About This Grant

Western SARE funds farmers and ranchers in the Western region to conduct on-farm research. Focus areas include water conservation, grazing management, soil health, and diversified cropping systems.

This program is specifically designed for working farmers who want to test sustainable practices on their own land. Projects should have clear, measurable outcomes that can be shared with other producers.
